About

Step Unit is a Milan-based music collective dedicated to creating immersive experiences bringing underground music to new contexts.

We curate events that push boundaries and connect artists with audiences in unique environments, from warehouse parties to brand activations.

Our mission is to foster a community that celebrates creative expression and cultural innovation in Milan's vibrant underground scene.
